Day 1 Itinerary For London Weekend Getaway: Transit
This map reflects my plan of action for the first day of my London Weekend Getaway, in terms of transit accessibility.
Each red circle is a tube station in London, the markers' are all stops on my first day in London, and the black circles are the size of the radius from the destination to the nearest tube station.
Click on the marker to see the stop number and location name. (The first two stops are the airports.)
